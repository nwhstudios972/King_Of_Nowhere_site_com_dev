## Geography

Stretches across the entire western slope of the northern mountains. Mild climate year-round, gentle winters. Ideal for the first hours of play.

## Inhabitants

- The elven clan of **Lirëan**
- A few human hermits
- A discreet angelic garrison along the northern edge

## Tips

Walk by day, listen to the songs. Never cut an ancient oak without elven permission: you would become the target of the forest itself.
