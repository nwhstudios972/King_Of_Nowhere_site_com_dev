## Geography

Stretches across the center of the map toward the north, where [The Bridge of Worlds](/en/univers/environnement/le-pont-des-mondes) can be made out above. Mild climate year-round, gentle winters. Ideal for the first hours of play.

## Inhabitants

- The elven clan of **Lirëan**
- A few human hermits
- A discreet angelic garrison along the northern edge

## Tips

Walk by day, listen to the songs. Never cut an ancient oak without elven permission: you would become a target of the forest itself by night.
