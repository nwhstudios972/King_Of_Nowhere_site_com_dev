## Geography

Draktar occupies the southeast corner of the map, wedged between volcanic foothills and the cursed lands. It's naturally shielded to the west by Shavir and the Wolf mountains, and to the north by The Breach and the Bridge of Worlds, which form the only passage to the upper lands. The Cave of Torments serves as a buffer zone to the northwest, providing a steady flow of undead that patrol the realm's borders. There is no access from the south or the east — cliffs of solidified lava plunging into an abyssal void form an impenetrable natural wall.

## Inhabitants

Draktar's inhabitants are exclusively demons and their servants. They tolerate no outsider without reason — every visit is a transaction of power.

- Legion of the Throne
- Smiths of the Soul Forge
- Cult of the Bloodstone
- Abyssal Guard

⚠️ Raising your reputation in Draktar automatically lowers your reputation with the Celestial Palace and Alexia.

## Tips

- Never come alone below the Bridge of Worlds if you aren't a demon or Wolf ally — patrols attack in groups of 4 minimum.
- Wear fire protection before entering — fire resistance of 60% minimum is recommended, or you'll bleed HP permanently.
- Stay on the rock platforms — falling into a lava channel is instant death without specific gear (lost loot).
- Avoid the Soul Forge without an active quest — the zone is a trap for the curious.
