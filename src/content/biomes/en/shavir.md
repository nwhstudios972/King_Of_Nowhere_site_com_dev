## Geography

Shavir lies in the southwest of the map, backed against the foothills of the volcanic mountains that mark the western border of demon territory. To the east, the lava flows pour naturally down toward Draktar, forming a corridor of fire that links the two bastions of the demon side. To the north, the mountain passes open onto the war zone with Alexia — a natural but constantly contested border. The Cave of Torments lies to the east, forming with Shavir a pincer that encircles the southern accesses to the Bridge of Worlds. The city is perched on a rocky plateau, granting it a formidable natural defensive position.

## Tips

- Never go out at night without being allied — even high-level players get overwhelmed by the nightly packs. Stay at an inn or leave the zone before the night cycle.
- Wear acid-resistant armor — the corrosive rains silently damage gear and force you to constantly repair weapons and armor (-5% DPS and defense every 3h — stackable).
- Don't enter the Cathedral without an active quest — the lunar curse is one of the hardest debuffs to cure, only curable at Ravenshield or Alexia.
- Stay on the paved roads — the side alleys are ambush zones for elite Bloodfangs, nearly undetectable before it's too late.
- The Blood Moon is your indicator — when it pulses and grows brighter, the Full Moon Night is approaching. You have 10 minutes to gear up for war.. or take cover. (PvP and PvE event)
- Farm the Black Wolves during the day in the outer passes — they're slower and less aggressive outside the night cycle, and their pelts are the most profitable resource of the zone.
