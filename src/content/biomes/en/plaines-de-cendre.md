## Geography

A former sea, dried by demonic magic. Covers the entire eastern fringe of the continent. Constant winds, never a clear sky.

## Inhabitants

- Mobile demonic camps
- Desperate human scavengers
- A few intelligent undead who still remember being someone

## Tips

Do not go alone. Do not stay at night. And if you find a rose growing in the ash, **do not pick it**.
