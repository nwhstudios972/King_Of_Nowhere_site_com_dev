## Origin

Hatched from an egg found in the ruins of Vald-the-Burnt, the Shadow Raven belongs to no one — it chooses its bearer. Once bound, it never leaves their shoulder.

## Behavior

Silent, watchful. Croaks once when an invisible enemy approaches.
