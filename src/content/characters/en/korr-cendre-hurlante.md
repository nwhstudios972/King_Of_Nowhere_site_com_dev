> *"We lost the pack. Krohm built a house. I built a road. It's the same thing, except mine bleeds more."*

Korr is leaner than [Krohm](/en/univers/personnages/krohm-criniere-cendree/), quieter, more tense. His mane is ash-red, cropped on the sides, long at the back of the neck. A scar runs across his left cheek — *"a souvenir from something that shouldn't have teeth"*. He smells of cold and dried blood.

He walks **with three spectral wolves**. Not companions — **prey**. He killed them himself ten years ago, in a hunt that went wrong. Instead of leaving, they followed him. **Out of respect.** Korr doesn't know what to do with them. He walks with them.

## The cousin who didn't stay

When Krohm's pack fell at the First March, Korr was not with them — he was hunting up north. He arrived two weeks later. Krohm was already burying the dead, one by one.

Korr looked. He put a hand on Krohm's shoulder. He said nothing for six hours. Then he spoke:

*"You staying?"*

*"I'm staying."*

*"Then I'm leaving. If you protect, someone has to hunt."*

And he left.

The two cousins have **not spoken since** — forty years. Not out of anger. Out of tacit agreement. Each does his part.

## The hunt for aberrations

Korr swore to kill **every aberrant creature** born from GrandPa's Scar. Nameless things — impossible anatomies, ash-skin, voices that speak backwards. Things angels refuse to look at, and that demons find even a bit too ugly.

Korr has killed **1,247**. He keeps a notebook. No names — he draws **their teeth**, because that is what stays with him.

He says there are **too many** left.

## Character

Silent on the hunt — he can track for days without a word. **Howler in the tavern** — laughs loud, drinks hard, and tells hunting stories no one believes (and that are all true).

Never refuses a challenge. Despises trophy hunters who bring back teeth for the pose. Adores young players who aren't afraid to bleed.

For wolf guilds, **hunting with Korr** is the ultimate initiation — those who return with an aberration tooth they killed themselves gain a respected status for life. Many don't return.

## Spawn and movement

- **Roams near Scar biomes and Rift zones.** Appears where aberrations have been reported recently.
- **Gives hunting quests**: bring back the heads of specific aberrations (Scar bosses and mini-bosses).
- **Becomes a world boss** if you hunt his prey without permission. He says it's *"a matter of respect, not territory."* He kills anyway.

## Epic weapon drop — *Fang-Spear*

Composite spear forged by Korr himself. Black wood shaft. **Twelve aberration fangs** set along the iron head.

- **Inflicts permanent bleed on Scar creatures** — the wound does not heal, the aberration dies as it moves.
- Each kill with the spear **adds a fang** to the collection (up to 20 max). More fangs = more weight — bigger damage, lower speed.
- **Restriction**: does not work against ordinary angels or demons. It is made for things **that should not exist**.

## Epic skill drop — *Blood-Howl*

The wielder howls. **Their three spectral wolves appear** at their side for **90 seconds**.

- The wolves automatically target the greatest threat within 50m.
- They fight to the death. They die when the spell ends, or sooner if struck down.
- **One use per IRL day.**
- **Rare effect**: if the wielder honors Krohm at his inn within 24h, the wolves come back **immediately** available. Korr knows. He always smiles when someone has done it.

> *"My cousin has a hearth. I have a road. Both lead somewhere. His brings them back. Mine kills."*
