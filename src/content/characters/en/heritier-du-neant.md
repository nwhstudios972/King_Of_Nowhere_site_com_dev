> *"Every choice has a price, brother. Make the right one… for you."*

Born between the whisper of broken oaths and the caress of the void, he carries within him **the elegance of forgotten courts** and **the cruelty of the abysses**. Neither fully man, nor fully demon, he moves where the threads of fate cross — offering to each a choice… **that may not be one at all**.

*Fate observes. Choice decides.*

## The child who bent reality

The Heir of the Void was born, second child of [Rose](/en/univers/personnages/rose/) and the [Split King](/en/univers/personnages/roi-fendu/), **half-demon, half-elf** — inheriting elven grace and demonic malice **in equal parts**.

From his earliest years, he discovered that he possessed extraordinary powers. So he chose to use them **to amuse himself at the expense of mortals** — a power that allowed him to bend reality to his will **with a terrifying elegance**.

## The chessboard of the world

Unlike his sister [Blanche](/en/univers/personnages/blanche/) who sought order and justice, the Heir of the Void saw the universe as **a playground** — a chessboard where every creature was but a piece to be moved for his amusement.

He became **master of the treacherous word**, able to turn his phrases with such finesse that no one could ever catch him in a lie — **simply omitting the truths that inconvenienced him** with a charming smile.

## The irresistible one

He was feared among gods and mortals. But more importantly, **they loved him** — so irresistible was his charisma, so intoxicating his company.

He played with mortal destinies like a sadistic child. He manipulated creatures of every kind, including demons. He schemed with naive angels for his plans — creating chaotic situations just to see how people would react, **feeding on the disorder and the suffering in a way that was almost sensual**.

## The fraternal war

[Blanche](/en/univers/personnages/blanche/) saw in him **everything she fought**.

He saw her only as **a rigid opponent — too predictable, too dull**.

And that, perhaps, is **his only true lie**: pretending that the chaos he sows has no consequence, no hidden intention behind his games — when in truth, **something deeply wounded within him is desperately seeking to be noticed**.

## The secret beneath the smile

For the Heir of the Void was also a child of the Split King and of Rose. And **the part of him that could have been loved** remained forever trapped in his role of destroyer.

The laugh hides the crack. The charm masks the loneliness. **The choice he offers to others, he himself has never received.**
