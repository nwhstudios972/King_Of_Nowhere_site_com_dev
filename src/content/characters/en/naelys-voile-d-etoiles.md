> *"The stars do not lie. They just forget to say everything. That's where I come in."*

Naelys is small, slim, and walks **barefoot** even on sharp rocks — *"it's the rocks that learn not to cut"*. Her skin is pale, almost translucent at night, and her blue-black hair falls to her shoulders, never tied up. Around her neck she wears a **living astrolabe** that pulses softly when she speaks.

She never raises her voice. She never gets angry. She calculates.

## The hidden disciple

[GrandMa](/en/univers/personnages/grandma/) — Shin'aido — has taken almost no disciples in a thousand years. Once, maybe twice. The third time, **it was Naelys**.

At 80 years old (young for an elf), Naelys discovered **a flaw in GrandPa's Scar** that no one else had seen. Not even the angels. Not even Marie. She mapped its position on a parchment, rolled it up, and went to leave it **at Shin'aido's door** without leaving a name.

Shin'aido found her three days later, reading the stars while sitting in a river. She looked at her. She said: *"Do you know what you found?"*

Naelys answered: *"Something that should not exist. Therefore something that must be known."*

Shin'aido took her as a disciple that evening. **No one knows.** Not even GrandPa.

## The impossible mapping

GrandPa's Scar **is not stable**. It moves. It pulses. It bleeds in some places, scars over in others. Mapping the Scar is mapping a living being that does not want to be seen.

Naelys has spent **80 years** on her map. She estimates it at **39%**. She says what's left is the hard part.

Why? Because someone, or something, **is already using it**. And she wants to know who before it is too late.

## Character

Calm, precise, deep. Speaks softly to the stars like old colleagues — *"the red one up there is in a foul mood tonight"*. Refuses escorts. Accepts silent companions.

Despises kings who think they can buy a map. Adores travelers who bring back **one detail** she hadn't noted.

For neutral and elven guilds, **becoming Naelys's "Veil-Follower"** is a rare and prestigious title — it opens secret routes no one else can take. Angelic and demonic guilds would love to seize her map. Sahari has already tried. Naelys saw her coming from 800m away.

## Spawn and movement

- **Appears at key points of the Scar**, determined by the Rift's pulses. Position changes every IRL week.
- **Gives exploration quests**: bring back fragments of stars, read broken constellations in dungeons, escort a measurement to a Rift zone.
- **Becomes a world boss** if betrayed (rare — but those who have done so died in silence, traceless, 2km from the zone).

## Epic weapon drop — *Living Astrolabe*

An instrument-weapon. A slim bronze circle set with pulsing crystals. Held in hand, thrown in an arc.

- **Reveals all hidden spells in a 50m radius** (invisibility, illusions, magical ambushes — visible for 30 seconds).
- Used as a weapon, deals **pure magical damage** ignoring armor.
- **Restriction**: if the wielder lies three times in one IRL day, the astrolabe **closes** and refuses to serve for 24h.

## Epic skill drop — *Traveler's Step*

The wielder teleports to **any location they have already visited** on the map.

- **Once per IRL day.**
- The journey is instant, but the wielder arrives **silent and breathless** — no combat possible for 5 seconds.
- The astrolabe holds up to **20 destinations** in memory. The 21st replaces the oldest. *"Choose what you remember."*
