> *Born of the sacred union of Marie, Archangel of Light,*
> *and Bélzébut, Prince of the Abysses,*
> *the Split King bears within him the fracture of heavens and hells.*
> *Neither angel. Nor demon.*
> *But the bridge that bleeds between the two worlds.*

## The impossible union

Born of the impossible union between **an archangel and a demon** — between **Marie**, Archangel of Light, and **Belzebut**, Prince of the Abysses — the Split King (Roi Fendu) was a creature of contradictions.

**Half-blinding light, half-repulsive darkness.** A sight that burned the eyes and turned the stomach.

Endowed with a power that his father coveted — who manipulated him and turned his life into hell — and that terrified his own mother, the archangels were forced to disown him from the **Celestial Palace**. Rather than imprison him in the **Draktar**, they turned their backs in fear and sent him down to earth.

## A hundred years of kindness

For his **first hundred years**, a naive and gentle child, he tried to save humanity despite his monstrous appearance.

He offered his help. His compassion. His love.

**Every act of kindness was rejected** — with disgust, with violence. Humans fled him. Some wanted to kill him. Others took advantage of his emotional fragility.

And little by little, his angelic heart began to burn out.

## Four centuries of falling

**Four hundred years of rejection and betrayal** turned the giver into a predator, the compassionate into a pitiless monster.

The gentle child became a creature who killed without hesitation, stole, committed every sin with **icy indifference**, leaving behind him destruction and grief.

His demonic nature finally bloomed, smothering the angelic light that had once tried to shine.

## Rose, too late

Then came **Rose**.

When he was already deeply corrupted. When the darkness had almost entirely consumed what was left of him.

She saw, through his nightmarish appearance, **a trace of the wounded being he had been** — a glimmer of the divine and the human where all others saw only the abomination. Rose touched something within him he believed lost forever.

But this rediscovery of love came **too late**. For the Split King had changed too much, suffered too much. And even the love of a single woman could not erase **five hundred years of darkness** running through his veins.

## The lineage

From his union with Rose were born four children, in this order:

1. [**Blanche**](/en/univers/personnages/blanche/) — half-elf, half-archangel. The Guardian. Pure heritage of light.
2. [**The Heir of the Void**](/en/univers/personnages/heritier-du-neant/) — half-elf, half-demon. The manipulator. Player of destinies.
3. [**The Dishevelled King**](/en/univers/personnages/roi-decoiffe/) — half-archangel, half-demon, half-elf, half-human. The quadruple lineage. The enigmatic one.
4. [**Faël**](/en/univers/personnages/fael/) — half-demon, half-human, weakened archangel blood. The Reluctant Herald.

Four children. Four more fractures in the fabric of the world.
