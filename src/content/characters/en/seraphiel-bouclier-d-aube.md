> *"No one crosses. Not today. Not as long as I stand."*

Seraphiel never shouts. His voice carries without rising — low, settled, calm — and yet you hear it from the other end of the Bridge. His wings are white, scarred with long golden lines: every one a scar from a demonic blow he absorbed **without stepping back**.

He fights **on his knees**. Always. So as not to crush a civilian or a child of either side who might pass near him in the melee. And even on his knees, **he is taller than most major demons**.

## The Bridge's Oath

When [Marie](/en/univers/personnages/archange-marie/) sealed the door of the Core, she named Seraphiel to guard **another threshold**: the **Bridge of Worlds**, linking the Celestial Palace to the human lands. Where GrandPa's Scar nearly spread. Where, without him, major demons would already be marching on the cities of men.

Seraphiel swore, before Marie, **never to leave the Bridge**. He has not slept in three hundred years. He prays standing, eats standing, bleeds standing.

And **no one has crossed**.

## Refuses reinforcements

The Palace regularly sends him relief squads. He turns them away. Always.

*"If I need ten angels to hold this bridge, then I should fall and someone better should take my place. I hold. Send them back."*

This pride has nearly gotten him killed several times. But it is also why **angelic guilds fight to bear his name** as a banner. A guild called "the Dawn-Shields" forms and dissolves every year — surviving a siege at his side has become a rite of passage.

## Character

Patient. Unshakable. Refuses compliments, refuses pity, refuses breaks.

Knows the name of every major demon he has killed — he recites the list under his breath in the evening, like a prayer. Sixty-eight names.

For demonic guilds, **slaying Seraphiel publicly** has become the ultimate goal. For angelic guilds, **defending him** is worth more than any relic.

## Spawn and movement

- **Permanent boss** on the Bridge of Worlds — always present, never absent.
- **Moves on event** when a major angelic city is under siege. Walks on foot. Takes several IRL hours. That is the event.
- **Reinforced by nearby angel players** (up to +100% HP with 10 angels in the zone).

## Epic weapon drop — *Sharpened-Dawn*

A composite spear-shield. The shield carries **one of Marie's feathers** set into its metal heart — and it is this feather that makes it so hard to break.

- Blocks demonic spells with **50% reflection** (the attacker takes half the damage they intended).
- The spear extends 2m when the wielder is on the defensive.
- **Restriction**: if a major demon carries it, the feather dies and the weapon goes inert.

## Epic skill drop — *Solar Verse*

The wielder recites a verse that only the Dawn-Shields know. **A 20m zone of pure light** spreads around them for 8 seconds.

- Burns major demons for **15% HP per second**.
- Heals non-demon allies for 5% HP per second.
- **One use per IRL day.** Miss the target, wait 24 hours.
