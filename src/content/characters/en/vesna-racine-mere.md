> *"The tree remembers. You — you can lie."*

Vesna's skin is thin like young birch bark, and her eyes belong to an elf who has seen too many seasons to count. She speaks slowly. She listens long. She never judges out loud — the tree, it judges in silence.

## Twelve hundred years, and a war that should not have happened

Vesna is 1,200 years old. She was a **druid-judge** of the Silver Forest — a title that barely exists anymore, because the Silver Forest barely exists anymore.

Three hundred years ago, an **inter-elven war** burned her sacred grove. Not demons. Not angels. Her own brethren, divided over a succession question no one remembers now.

Vesna survived because she was presiding over a troll's trial when the fire took. She came back the next day. Nothing was left. Only warm ash and **a single seed** in the pocket of her robe — a mother-tree seed she always carried, *just in case*.

## The tree she bled

She planted the seed in the center of the ashes. Then she cut her palm, and she fed the seed her blood. **Every day. For three hundred years.**

The tree came up slowly. Very slowly. It grew crooked, memory in the wood, voice in the sap. Vesna eventually carved her first room into the trunk, then a second, then a bedroom, then a counter. **The inn became the tree. The tree became the inn.**

The walls breathe. The beams retract in winter. The roof grows a few centimeters in spring. Vesna prunes it with her good hand.

## Character

Slow, attentive, infinitely patient. Recognizes a liar **by the smell of their sweat** — a judge's gift, not magic, just a thousand years of attention.

Despised by young elves who find her old-fashioned and too neutral. Revered by elders who still remember the Silver Forest. She does not seek to please or to convince. She pours drinks, she listens, she tidies.

She hates oaths sworn lightly. If you swear under her roof, **you swear to the tree**. And the tree remembers.

## Exclusive ability — *Whispers to the Roots*

Everything spoken aloud under the roof of **The Watchful Stump** is **recorded by the tree**. Vesna can, in exchange for a service rendered (not gold — a service), retrieve a conversation heard days, months, or even years ago.

Mechanic:
- The player requests a "root consultation" and offers a service in exchange.
- Vesna places her hand on the central trunk, closes her eyes, and recites word for word what was said.
- **Limit**: she can only consult once per IRL lunar month. The service must be proportional to the secret retrieved.

That is why diplomats, spies, and jealous spouses all, one day, end up crossing her door.

## Sanctuary

- **Indestructible**: to destroy the inn, you would have to fell the tree. To fell the tree, you would have to kill Vesna. No one has ever succeeded — not through power, through patience: Vesna is *more patient than war*.
- The roots extend 500 meters around the inn. Anyone approaching with clear intent to harm **is slowed** as if walking through sap.
- Any fire set against the walls dies within 3 seconds. The tree has learned not to burn anymore.

> *"They burned my forest. I burned my patience. That is all I have left. And it is more than enough."*
