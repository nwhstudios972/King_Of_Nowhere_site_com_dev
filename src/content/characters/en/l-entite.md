## Formless, faceless

The Entity has no fixed form. It manifests differently to whoever perceives it — but always with one constant: **a cold, emotionless light** that emanates from it.

## In the beginning

In the beginning there was nothing. Then the Entity simply **was**, without reason, without intent. It created the worlds and the beings — not out of love, but out of mere existence, out of the very nature of what it is.

Humans emerged from its creation, conscious and searching for meaning, but the Entity remained indifferent to their prayers. **Source of infinite power**, it would offer our valiant heroes the choice to embody either an evil or divine power.

The Entity observed all of this without ever intervening, without ever judging — like a natural, immutable force.

## The illusion of civilisations

Civilisations rose and fell, convinced they understood the divine will. It was always an illusion.

Through the ages, the Entity became myth, legend, concept — but it always remained the same: **impersonal and eternal source of power, blessing or cursing a being while leaving them free** to live the existence they chose for themselves.

## What the last sages understood

The last sages began to understand that perhaps the Entity had never wanted anything from us. And yet we exist, we evolve, we suffer, we build — **simply because it is the source that makes us more powerful**.

Maybe that is the true blessing, or the true curse: this absolute indifference in the face of our infinite hunger for power.

## The Core of the Entity

The Entity dwells in **The Core** — a unique in-game environment, the point of origin and return of all power in King Of Nowhere. There the heaviest choices are made, there the deepest oaths are sealed.

> *It is, and that is enough.*
