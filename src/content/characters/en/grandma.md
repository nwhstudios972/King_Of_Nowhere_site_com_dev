> *"I am not time. I am the one who remembers it."*

Ancient among the ancients, GrandMa is **the living soul of the forests** and **the memory of worlds**. She has watched the stars be born and the elements dance. Through her infinite wisdom, she keeps the balance between life and the void.

It is said that when she speaks, **nature listens**. When she acts, **worlds harmonise**.

*Nature. Wisdom. Elements. Eternity.*

## Shin'aido, witness of creation

Her true name is **Shin'aido**. The wise elf.

She lived **from the creation of the worlds onwards**, witness of the birth of the universe and all its wonders — guided by a timeless wisdom and a deep love for art and beauty in every form.

## The silent blessing

[The Entity](/en/univers/personnages/l-entite/), recognising the magnificent and selfless acts she carried out **in secret for the good of all**, granted her the gift of immortality — and blessed her with extraordinary powers **only she truly understood**.

She kept her youth and her beauty across the ages, becoming a legendary figure among the elves — revered and exalted as **a living goddess**, a queen of wisdom and charisma.

## The strategic marriage

When she met **Grand-Pa**, she saw in him a means to unite the forgotten worlds — the elves and the humans — to forge an alliance capable of withstanding the storms to come.

She agreed to marry him under the pretence of merely wishing to help humanity. But in truth, **it was a carefully planned strategy** to rally the causes — a noble mission hidden behind the appearance of a simple romance.

## The staff that shakes the earth

GrandMa carried **an ancient staff**, an artefact that allowed her to make the earth itself tremble — a power none suspected, **a secret kept deep within her warrior's heart**.

With it at her side, she continued her magnificent works in silence, guiding peoples toward peace, **orchestrating events with a subtlety only the wisest could perceive**.

## The king's anchor

She became far more than a mere companion to Grand-Pa. She was **his guide, his anchor, the silent force** behind every one of his great decisions.

And though all respected her for her eternal beauty and intoxicating charisma, **few knew that GrandMa carried upon her shoulders the burden of millennia** — and that each day she chose to continue helping, **without ever asking for recognition**.

## Queen of two worlds

And so the ancient wise elf became **queen of the humans** — uniting two worlds through love and strategy, through charisma and secret.

And in the silence of her forests, **Shin'aido continues to remember**.
