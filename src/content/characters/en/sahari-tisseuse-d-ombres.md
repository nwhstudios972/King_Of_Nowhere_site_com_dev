> *"See that waitress at the back of the room? No, don't turn around. She's been watching you for twenty minutes. That's me."*

Sahari stands 1m70 — unless she needs to be something else. She changes face, voice, gait, perfume. Her true form — when she remembers it — is a tall ink-black-skinned demoness, with eyes **empty and bright** like two mirrors of jet.

She wears a smoke-gray veil woven from her own shadows. When she takes it off, **you forget her face within three seconds**. It is a curse she paid dearly for. And she adores it.

## A year with the Heir of the Void

Sahari was a minor demoness, talented, ambitious. She requested an audience with [the Heir of the Void](/en/univers/personnages/heritier-du-neant/). He received her. He looked at her. He smiled.

*"You want to learn? I give you a year. Not a day more. If you come out changed, you'll be the most dangerous in Draktar. If you come out broken, that's your fault."*

She lasted the year. She came out **changed**. And she became exactly what he had said — except she added something he had never had: **patience**.

He played for pleasure. She plays to win.

## The hunt for deserters

Belzébut recruited her personally when Thévalk laid down his insignia. The King of Demons needed someone who was neither a soldier (too visible) nor a general (too political). Someone invisible.

Her official mission: **track, identify, and bring back alive every demonic deserter**.

Her unofficial mission: bring back **Thévalk** with extra stitches. Belzébut forbade killing him — but he did not forbid **adding seams**. Sahari is waiting for her moment.

She has already returned **seventeen deserters** to the vaults of Draktar. None escaped a second time.

## The secrets

Sahari knows **one secret about every demonic noble in Draktar**. Not through blackmail — through utility. She doesn't pressure. She lets them know she knows. *It's more elegant.*

She also knows secrets about angels. She doesn't use them. *"Why waste a beautiful secret by using it?"*

For demonic guilds, she is **the dream and the nightmare**. Working with her opens every door — betraying her means dying in your sleep, **with a smile on your face**.

For angelic guilds, she is **the ultimate spy to bring down** — and no one has succeeded.

## Character

Elegant, seductive, glacial. Lies without blinking. Can listen for three hours without saying a word. Loves fine ironies. Despises brutes like [Vargoth](/en/univers/personnages/vargoth-couronne-de-suie/) — *"Shouting to win is admitting you didn't think first."*

Has one known weakness: **cats**. No one knows why. She always stops to pet a cat. Even mid-mission.

## Spawn and movement

- **Appears on event** when a demon player has helped an angel or a neutral (even indirectly). The event is silent — no announcement, no bell. The player learns she is here by meeting someone they knew, but who doesn't quite have the right voice.
- **City infiltration**: may appear disguised in any neutral city. Recognizable only by a minor detail (scent, accent, gesture).
- **Assassination boss**: if an angelic guild correctly identifies her, she reveals herself and fights alone. She never calls Vargoth for help. That is beneath her.

## Epic weapon drop — *Veil-of-Thousand-Eyes*

A dagger-scarf. A 20cm slim blade attached to a black veil woven from living shadows.

- **Grants 6 seconds of invisibility** after each kill (chained-ambush mechanic).
- The veil **records the faces** of the last five opponents struck — the wielder can briefly imitate their appearance (up to 10 seconds).
- **Restriction**: an angel who touches it is blinded for 30 seconds. The blade then folds itself and refuses to serve.

## Epic skill drop — *Step into the Rift*

Short teleport through walls and obstacles. Range **15m**. **3 charges**, full recharge in 5 IRL minutes.

- During the teleport, the wielder is **invulnerable and invisible**.
- On arrival, they suffer **3 seconds of forced silence** — cannot speak or cast. Sahari's deliberate drawback: *"If you slipped through, shut up. Don't draw attention."*
