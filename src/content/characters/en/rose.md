> *"Sometimes the softest light shows the darkest path."*

Born of a forgotten heritage, Rose walks between two worlds. Quiet but deeply empathic, she heals the wounded and the heartbroken with a single word, a look, or a flower. She chooses compassion where others choose power. **Her true strength lies in the love she gives in silence.**

## The forgotten heritage

Rose was a **half-elf princess without knowing it**, born of a mother she never knew, raised alone within the castle walls while her father [**Grandpa**](/en/univers/personnages/grandpa/) waged ceaseless war.

Despite this deep solitude, she radiated compassion, walking her kingdom to bring peace and smiles to those who suffered — as if she were unconsciously trying to fill the void of her absent mother.

## The sacred clearing

One day, in the **sacred clearing**, she met the **Split King** as he stared at his own reflection in a lake. And in that moment suspended outside of time, **two broken souls finally recognised each other**.

They spoke for days while time stood still. Rose saw, beyond the physical abomination, **a heart as wounded as her own**, still capable of loving.

## The double life

Despite the violent refusal of [Grandpa](/en/univers/personnages/grandpa/) — who knew the monstrous nature of the Split King — Rose continued **in secret** to meet him. And from those meetings were born **four children**, impossible fruits of a forbidden love:

1. [**Blanche**](/en/univers/personnages/blanche/) — half-elf, half-archangel. The Guardian.
2. [**The Heir of the Void**](/en/univers/personnages/heritier-du-neant/) — half-elf, half-demon. The manipulator.
3. [**The Dishevelled King**](/en/univers/personnages/roi-decoiffe/) — half-archangel, half-demon, half-elf, half-human. The enigmatic one.
4. [**Faël**](/en/univers/personnages/fael/) — half-demon, half-human, weakened archangel blood. The Reluctant Herald.

She lived a double life: **a princess hiding her criminal happiness, secret mother of children the world was never meant to see**.

## The sacrifice

But when the Split King fell into a fury and threatened to destroy the world, Rose placed herself **between him and destruction**, desperately trying to wake the man she loved within him.

Her cry of love unleashed a rage so primal that the **Split King's uncontrolled power burned her along with the entire city**, freezing the black fire around her.

And in his smoking arms, the Split King understood that he had just killed **the only person who had ever truly seen him** — the only light that could have saved him — and that his four children would now be orphans of the only love they would ever know.

> *Sometimes the softest light shows the darkest path. But sometimes, it is also the first to be put out.*
