> *A king became, against his own will, the destroyer of worlds.*

Grandpa, **king of men**, stands majestic at two metres tall, bearing an ancient and wise gaze. With his silver hair, he embodies **wisdom and strength**. Beloved by his whole kingdom, his heart is as vast as his dreams.

His kindness is sung by his subjects, and his legend echoes through the ages. **He is an epic king, a pillar of justice in trial, and a light of hope for all.**

What is sung of him is true. And that is what makes his fall unbearable.

## The beloved king

For years, Grand-Pa ruled with **honour and compassion**, forging alliances, protecting his people — becoming a legendary figure of just governance even other realms deeply respected.

By his side, [GrandMa](/en/univers/personnages/grandma/) — the silent anchor behind his greatest decisions. To him, simply a cherished companion. To her, a millennial mission hidden in plain sight.

## The manipulation of the archangels

But **the archangels**, watching the [Split King](/en/univers/personnages/roi-fendu/) growing in power and chaos, decided he had to be eliminated.

And they manipulated Grand-Pa — convincing him it was **the only way to save the universe** from inevitable destruction.

They orchestrated a secret meeting. There, Grand-Pa was persuaded to **drink the blood of demons** — an unnatural alliance that would transform him into a warrior able to rival the half-archangel, half-demon creature.

In his certainty that it was for the greater good, Grand-Pa accepted. **Utterly unaware** that the archangels merely sought to create a living weapon — **a human bomb** against their enemy.

## The fury and the battle

When the [Split King](/en/univers/personnages/roi-fendu/) fell into fury and threatened to destroy the world, Grand-Pa charged into the battle.

**Transformed by the demonic blood** now flowing through his veins. Blinded by vengeance and the certainty of doing good.

## The scar in reality

In fighting the Split King, Grand-Pa unwittingly created **a catastrophic rift between worlds** — a scar in reality itself that would forever upend the balance of the universe.

## The truth about Rose's death

And it was in this battle that [Rose](/en/univers/personnages/rose/) perished.

**Not killed by her lover the Split King**, as all believed — as the Split King himself convinced himself forever — but **caught in the chaos of the wars created by Grand-Pa himself**.

The one who should have protected her.

Her father.

## The realisation, too late

Grand-Pa realised too late that he had been manipulated. That his noble intention had birthed **the greatest catastrophe the universe had ever known**.

His wife, a hidden goddess. His daughter, dead by his own hand. His bloodline, broken. His kingdom, torn by the rift.

## And so the game began

And so **the game of King Of Nowhere began**.

Upon the ruins of a well-intentioned but deeply devastating decision.

The moment when **a king became, against his own will, the destroyer of worlds**.

And the moment when it falls to others — to the players, to the Elites, to the souls you will embody — to decide what becomes of the ruins.
