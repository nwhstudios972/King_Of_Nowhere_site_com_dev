## Origin

It is said they were born from the tears of a broken moon. The Elves see them as kin and refuse to hunt them. The Demons hate them: their gaze unbinds the oaths of fire.

## In game

Rare spawn in the **Silver Forest**, usually alone. Tactical combat: lateral dodges, deep bites, flees if wounded below 30%. Killing a Silver Wolf in an Elven zone causes a lasting reputation loss.
