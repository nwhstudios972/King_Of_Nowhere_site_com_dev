## Origin

Ash Stalkers were once ordinary villagers. When the Demons poured black fire upon the plains, their bodies burned but their oaths — still unkept — kept them from dying. Since then, they wander.

## In game

They appear in groups of 3 to 7 in the **Ash Plains** between dusk and dawn. Slow when walking, they charge at short range as soon as they detect a living heat source. Avoid without a blessed torch.
