## Lore

When Archangel Marie swore the first oath at the foot of the dawn altar, her tear fell onto a raw blade. The metal sang. Since then, **every Dawn Blade** is forged by repeating that oath, and only awakens for those who have sworn sincerely.

## In-game

- High damage against **Demon**-faction units
- Passive: illuminates a 10m radius around the wielder, reveals invisibles
- Penalty: -20% damage against neutral units
