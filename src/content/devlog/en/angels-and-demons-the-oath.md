## Not just an armor color

Factions are often treated as interchangeable palettes: you pick red or blue, the gameplay doesn't change. King Of Nowhere goes the other way.

Choosing the **Angels** means accepting a moral code, magic limits, forced alliances with other orders. It also unlocks blessings you can't get anywhere else.

Choosing the **Demons** means freeing yourself from all moral restraint — but it also means you can never enter certain sanctuaries, and you'll have to betray someone, sooner or later.

## The server balance counts

If too many souls tilt to one side, the server's balance shifts. Sacred regions fall, mechanics collapse, others emerge.

Your choice is never isolated.
