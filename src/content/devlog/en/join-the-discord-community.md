## An independent studio, a long-term bet

Nowhere Studios is an independent studio. No big publisher behind us, no deadline forced by a shareholder. That means two things: we keep our hands on the vision — and **we need you** to keep the journey going.

Supporting us means letting us keep building slowly, cleanly, without selling out our choices.

## Why a Discord?

Because an MMORPG built by its players cannot be designed behind closed doors. We want to test, show, adjust — with you, live.

On the Discord you'll find:

- **Previews** before anyone else — concepts, prototypes, construction shots.
- **Votes** that actually weigh on design decisions.
- **Open conversations** with the team, not cosmetic community management.
- **Bugs** and feedback that go straight into our sprints.

## Iterating together, at every time scale

We have one obsession: thinking of this world as a living project that lasts.

- **In 1 week**: which mechanic we prototype, which feedback we fold into the next build.
- **In 1 month**: which slice of the world we open, which faction we deepen, which race we unveil.
- **In 1 year**: what the first live season looks like, which guilds will have left a mark on history.
- **In 10 years**: what King Of Nowhere will have become — a world you are the true authors of.

That horizon, we can't write alone.

## How to join us

👉 [Join the official Discord](#discord)

If you believe in what we're building, tell the people around you. Every soul that pushes the door makes this world a little more real.

Thank you. Truly.
