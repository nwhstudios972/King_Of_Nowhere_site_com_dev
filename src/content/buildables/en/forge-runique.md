## Use

Allows the crafting of any weapon, armor or object using stellar iron. Only one forge can be active within a 30-meter radius — beyond that, the runes interfere.

## Tiers

- **Tier 1**: common and uncommon weapons.
- **Tier 2**: rare weapons (requires an angelic or demonic altar nearby).
- **Tier 3**: epic and legendary weapons (requires an Archangel or Archdemon nearby at craft time).
