## Use

The base brick of any serious construction. Stackable vertically, shapeable into arches, towers, ramparts. Guilds use them by the tens of thousands to raise whole cities.

## Specifics

- Can be engraved with a protective rune (+25% durability).
- Visibly cracks at 50% HP, warning defenders.
- Demons with access to the Cursed Forge can make it bleed — purely cosmetic, but unsettling.
