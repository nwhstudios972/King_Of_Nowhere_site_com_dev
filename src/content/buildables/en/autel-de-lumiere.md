## Use

Lets a player swear or renew their angelic oath. The ritual takes 10 seconds; leaving it early has no effect, but marks the character as "undecided" for 24 in-game hours.

## Passive effects

- Slowly heals all Angels within a 50-meter radius.
- Repels minor demonic creatures.
- Prevents shadow teleport within its aura.
