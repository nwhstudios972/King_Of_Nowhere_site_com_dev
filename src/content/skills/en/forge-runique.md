## Description

The Runic Forge separates the blacksmith from the true artisan. A raw blade cuts; a runic blade burns, freezes, or screams.

## Learning

- Master Orven, at the Vald sanctuary, teaches the first runes in exchange for a favor
- The elves of the clearing share protection runes with those who prove their respect for the trees
