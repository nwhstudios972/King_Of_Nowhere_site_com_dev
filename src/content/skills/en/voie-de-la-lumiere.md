## Description

The Path of Light lets the caster channel the primordial clarity of the angelic oath. It is fed by the caster's faith and by daylight.

## Core spells

- **Dawn Ray** — heals a friendly target
- **Oath Shield** — temporary protection
- **Revelation** — cancels invisibility effects in an area

## Limits

Light spells weaken on moonless nights and in cursed biomes.
