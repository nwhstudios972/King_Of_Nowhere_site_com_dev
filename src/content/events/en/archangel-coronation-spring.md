## The throne is empty

After the fall of the previous Archangel (see the May 15 devlog), the Council of Light has opened nominations. Three players have been retained — their names will be revealed 24 hours before the ceremony.

## Order of events

- **6:00 PM** — Cathedral doors open
- **7:00 PM** — The three contenders are presented
- **7:30 PM** — Ritual duel (Council's judgment, non-lethal)
- **8:30 PM** — Coronation and first edict
- **9:00 PM** — Public feast on the steps (community loot for everyone in attendance)

## Why come

This isn't a cutscene. It's a live event, played by players. Your cheers, your oaths, your silences will be written into the server's history.
