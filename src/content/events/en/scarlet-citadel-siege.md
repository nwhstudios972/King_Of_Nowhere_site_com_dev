## The seasonal siege

Once a season, the Scarlet Citadel opens its gates to the siege. Angels want to bring it down. Demons want to keep it. Independents can sell their blades to either side — until they pick one.

## Sign-ups

Guilds must register at least **72 hours before** the siege opens, through *Herald of the Council* (Angel capital) or *Shadow Marshal* (Demon capital).

## Rewards

- **Winning side** : season mark + treasury access for 7 days
- **Top 3 guilds** (by contribution) : permanent banner, hung from the Citadel until the next siege
- **Individual MVP** : legendary title, visible across every server
