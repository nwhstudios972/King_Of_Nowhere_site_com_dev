## The moon bleeds, the world stirs

Every year, on the first red moon of summer, the world of King Of Nowhere slips into a ritual interlude. Wolves shift earlier. Neutral altars accept rare offerings. Wandering merchants unpack relics you almost never see.

## What changes during the festival

- **Drop boost** on Moon relics (×2 across every biome)
- **Seasonal quest** : *The Oath of the She-Wolf* — three tiers, ten days to complete it
- **Ritual merchants** appear in the four capitals (different spot every day)
- **Festive look** granted to every Wolf who logs in at least once during the window

## Why join

Festival rewards only return once a year. And some items don't even transfer — you have to be there, that night, on that server.
